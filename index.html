Our Origin

Our Story: From Memory to Masterpiece

ByToothless began as a hobby when I was thinking of a perfect gift for my loved ones. When I was given a beautiful bouquet of flowers, I realized I needed a way to preserve that feeling forever.

"It wasn't just a bouquet; it was a memory I couldn't bear to let fade."

That passion turned into a business idea: Why should finding a special gift be hard? I discovered resin—the most effective way to keep moments alive.
